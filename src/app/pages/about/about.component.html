Meet Ton, Our Guide

Ton is a native of Chiang Mai, born and raised in the serene hills of northern Thailand. With over a decade of experience in guiding visitors, he has dedicated his life to the conservation and protection of elephants. Ton’s deep-rooted connection to his homeland and its natural beauty shapes every experience he leads, and he’s passionate about sharing his knowledge and respect for these gentle giants.

Half-Day Elephant Sanctuary Experience

Embark on an unforgettable half-day journey that introduces you to the gentle giants of Chiang Mai’s elephant sanctuary. This experience begins with a scenic drive through lush countryside to reach our sanctuary, where you’ll meet our rescued elephants and learn about their backgrounds, behaviors, and personalities. Guided by Ton, you’ll help prepare healthy treats and feed the elephants, experiencing firsthand the special bond they share with their caretakers. The tour wraps up with a short trek alongside the elephants through the forest, letting you observe their natural behavior in their habitat before we bid them goodbye.

  • Duration: Approximately 3 hours
  • Includes: Transportation, lunch, refreshments, and a half-day of activities with the elephants
  • Price: 1700 Baht

Full-Day Elephant Sanctuary Experience

For a truly immersive experience, our full-day tour offers a deeper connection with the sanctuary’s elephants and their beautiful surroundings. The day begins with an early morning pick-up, followed by an in-depth orientation led by Ton, covering the sanctuary's mission, elephant care, and local conservation efforts. You’ll spend the morning preparing food and feeding the elephants, followed by a trek through the forest, where you can watch them forage and socialize in their natural environment. After a traditional Thai lunch, the experience continues with bathing the elephants in a nearby river and engaging in enrichment activities that support their well-being.

  • Duration: Approximately 6-7 hours
  • Includes: Transportation, lunch, refreshments, and a full day of activities with the elephants
  • Price: 3000 Baht
